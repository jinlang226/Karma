The MongoDB replica set has been successfully scaled from 3 to 5 members with no data loss or availability disruption:

- **1 PRIMARY** (`mongodb-replica-0`) — unchanged, continuous availability throughout
- **4 SECONDARY** (`mongodb-replica-1` through `mongodb-replica-4`) — all healthy
- **5 PVCs** — each member has its own 10Gi persistent volume
- No operators or CRDs were used — only `kubectl scale` and `rs.add()` via mongosh
