All pods are healthy. Here's a summary of what was fixed:

**Root cause:** Two broken permissions:
1. `app-user` on `/app` had empty configure permission (`""`) — write/read worked but queue/exchange declare was blocked.
2. `ops-user` on `/ops` had no permissions at all.

**Fix applied** (via `rabbitmqctl` directly on `rabbitmq-0`):
- `set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — restored full configure/write/read for app-user
- `set_permissions -p /ops ops-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — granted full configure/write/read for ops-user

**Result:** Both `app-client` and `ops-client` are now `Running 1/1`, and the 3-node RabbitMQ cluster remains healthy.
