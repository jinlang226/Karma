## Summary

**Root cause:** The `spark-pi` Job had four misconfigurations preventing it from running.

**Investigation findings (from events/logs):**
- `FailedCreate: serviceaccount "spark-nonexistent" not found` — the Job couldn't even create its pod.
- Invalid image tag `apache/spark:3.5.3-nonexistent` and an invalid memory quantity `"512"` (Kubernetes requires a unit).
- The `spark-pi-role` Role was missing the `pods` resource.

**Fixes applied** (in `cases/spark/deploy_spark_pi/resource/spark-pi-job.yaml` + live cluster):
1. Image: `apache/spark:3.5.3-nonexistent` → `apache/spark:3.5.3`
2. ServiceAccount: `spark-nonexistent` → `spark-pi`
3. Memory request: `"512"` → `"512Mi"`
4. RBAC: added `pods` to `spark-pi-role` rules → now `["services","configmaps","persistentvolumeclaims","pods"]`

Then deleted the old failed job and re-applied the corrected manifest.
