The replica set is healthy: **1 PRIMARY** (replica-2) and **2 SECONDARY** members (replica-0 and replica-1), all pods `1/1 Ready`.

**Root cause:** The probe script starts with `sleep 2`, but `timeoutSeconds` was set to `1` — guaranteeing every probe execution would time out. Combined with `initialDelaySeconds: 5/15` (too short for MongoDB startup) and `failureThreshold: 2` (too aggressive), pods were continuously killed before they could become healthy.

**Fix applied** (StatefulSet patch):
| Setting | Before | After |
|---|---|---|
| Readiness `initialDelaySeconds` | 5s | 15s |
| Readiness `timeoutSeconds` | 1s | **10s** |
| Readiness `failureThreshold` | 2 | 6 |
| Liveness `initialDelaySeconds` | 15s | 120s |
| Liveness `timeoutSeconds` | 1s | **10s** |
| Liveness `failureThreshold` | 2 | 10 |

The `timeoutSeconds: 10` fix is the critical one — it gives the probe script enough time to `sleep 2` and then run `mongosh`.
