## Summary

**Root cause:** The `app-user` had an **empty `configure` permission** in the `/app` vhost. The producer needs `configure` rights to *declare* a queue, so its `QueueDeclare` was rejected with `not_authorised` → CrashLoopBackOff.

**Fix applied** (durable, no data loss):
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```
This grants the missing `configure` permission (keeping existing write/read). After a `rollout restart` of `app-producer`, it stays up and publishes successfully.
