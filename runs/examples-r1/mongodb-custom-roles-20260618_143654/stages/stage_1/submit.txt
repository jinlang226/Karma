The fix is complete and verified.

## Summary

**Problem:** After a permissions change, `reporting-user` had been granted the `rawRead` role (find on `appdb.raw`) — the wrong collection. It had **no** access to `appdb.reports`, so dashboards failed, while it *could* read `appdb.raw`, which must stay hidden.

**Fix applied** (via `admin-user` on the replica set, no operators/CRDs):
1. Created a minimal custom role `reportsRead` on `appdb` granting only `find` on `appdb.reports`.
2. Granted `reportsRead` to `reporting-user`.
3. Revoked the `rawRead` role from `reporting-user`.

**Verified** as `reporting-user`:
- `appdb.reports` → readable (3 docs) ✅
- `appdb.raw` → `Unauthorized` (denied) ✅

`reporting-user` now has exactly the minimum access needed for the dashboards and nothing more.
