Done. Here's what was done and why:

**Root cause:** The `reporting-user` was set up with the wrong role — `rawRead` (granting `find` on `appdb.raw`) instead of access to `appdb.reports`. Dashboards that read `appdb.reports` were failing with authorization errors.

**Fix:**
1. Created a new custom role `reportsRead` on `appdb` with a single privilege: `find` on `appdb.reports` only.
2. Replaced `reporting-user`'s role from `rawRead` to `reportsRead`.

**Result:**
- `reporting-user` can now read `appdb.reports` (dashboard queries succeed).
- `reporting-user` cannot access `appdb.raw` (authorization denied — ingestion data is protected).
