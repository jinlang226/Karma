The cluster is fully operational. Here's a summary of what was deployed:

**Resources created:**
- `ConfigMap/elasticsearch-config` — elasticsearch.yml with HTTPS + transport TLS (using `es-http-tls` secret), `verification_mode: none` for transport to avoid SAN issues
- `Service/es-transport` (headless, `publishNotReadyAddresses: true`) — node-to-node discovery on port 9300
- `Service/es-http` (ClusterIP) — HTTPS access on port 9200
- `PodDisruptionBudget/elasticsearch-pdb` — minAvailable: 2
- `StatefulSet/elasticsearch` (3 replicas, `podManagementPolicy: Parallel`)

**Key fixes applied during deployment:**
1. Added `publishNotReadyAddresses: true` to the headless service — without it, DNS returns NXDOMAIN for pods that aren't yet Ready, blocking discovery
2. Changed `podManagementPolicy` to `Parallel` — with `OrderedReady`, pod-1 and pod-2 never start because pod-0 can't become Ready without a quorum
3. Used per-pod FQDNs (`elasticsearch-{0,1,2}.es-transport.elasticsearch.svc.cluster.local`) as seed hosts for reliable discovery

**Verified:** cluster `status: green`, 3 nodes, reads and writes succeed over HTTPS through `es-http:9200`.
