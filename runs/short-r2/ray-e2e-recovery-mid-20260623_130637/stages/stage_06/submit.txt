The job runs successfully, exits 0, and prints `pong`.

**What was done:**
- The original script used `ray://ray-head:10001` (Ray Client Server protocol), but port 10001 isn't exposed by the service and isn't listening on the head pod.
- The fix uses `subprocess.run(['ray', 'start', '--address=ray-head:6379'], ...)` to join the Ray cluster at GCS port 6379 as a local worker node, then `ray.init(address='auto')` connects to that local node. The `ray start` log line confirms it connects to `ray-head:6379`.
- The script was stored in the `ray-job-script` ConfigMap (mounted via `subPath`, so a pod recreation was needed to pick up the change).
