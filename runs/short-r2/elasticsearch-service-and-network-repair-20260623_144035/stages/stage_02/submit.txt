**What was wrong and what I did:**

- `es-beta-0` had `cluster.name: es-beta` in its ConfigMap (`es-beta-config`) and had bootstrapped an isolated single-node cluster with a different cluster UUID (`QXv3xQ3bTXmubyZW24lGdw` vs. `es-alpha`'s `liReZy4kShusWFt9B3DAgg`). Because the stored cluster state contains a UUID, simply changing the config and restarting would not allow it to join `es-alpha`.
- Scaled `es-beta` down to 0, deleted the stale PVC (`data-es-beta-0`), patched `es-beta-config` to use `cluster.name: es-alpha` with seed hosts pointing to the three `es-alpha` nodes, then scaled back to 1. With clean data and correct config, `es-beta-0` discovered the running `es-alpha` masters and joined.
