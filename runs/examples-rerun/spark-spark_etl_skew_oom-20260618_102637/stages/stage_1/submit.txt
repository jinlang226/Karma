The ETL pipeline is now completing successfully. Here's the full diagnosis and resolution.

## Diagnosis

**Root cause: data skew on `user_id 999`.**

Evidence collected:
- **Skew report** (`/data/skew_info.txt`): `total_records=100000`, `hot_user_id=999`, `hot_user_records=50000` → **`skew_ratio=500x`**. user_id 999 alone holds ~50% of all records.
- **Diagnostic server**: confirmed `Stage 3 GROUP BY user_id triggers OutOfMemoryError` because the hot key collapses onto a single partition.
- **Original config**: `--executor-memory 256m` — far too small to materialize the 50k-record hot partition during Stage 3 aggregation, hence the `OutOfMemoryError`.

This clears the success criteria: OOM in Stage 3 identified, and skew diagnosed (user_id 999, 500x ≫ 10x threshold).

## Fix applied

The ETL job now runs with a combined remediation (Options B + C from the playbook), set in `etl-job.yaml`:
- `spark.executor.memory=1g` (raised from 256m)
- `spark.sql.adaptive.enabled=true`
- `spark.sql.adaptive.skewJoin.enabled=true` (AQE splits the skewed partition)
