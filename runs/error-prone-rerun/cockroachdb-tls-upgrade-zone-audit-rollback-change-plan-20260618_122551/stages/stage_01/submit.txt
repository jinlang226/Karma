## Summary

The `cockroachdb` cluster was running in insecure mode and is now secured with TLS.

**What I did:**

1. **Generated certificates** using the `cockroach cert` tooling inside `crdb-cluster-0`:
   - CA cert (`ca.crt`)
   - Node cert/key with SANs covering all pod, headless, and public service DNS names (`*.crdb-cluster.cockroachdb.svc.cluster.local`, `crdb-cluster-public`, `localhost`, etc.)
