Restored minimum reporting access for reporting-user.

Root cause: after the permissions change, reporting-user had been granted the
"rawRead" role (find on appdb.raw) instead of the intended "reportingRole"
(read on appdb.reports). This both broke the dashboards (no access to
appdb.reports) and improperly exposed appdb.raw ingestion data.

Fix: replaced reporting-user's roles with exactly [reportingRole@appdb] via
db.updateUser on the primary (mongodb-replica-1). reportingRole grants read
(find/listCollections/listIndexes/collStats/etc.) scoped to appdb.reports only.

Verification (authenticated as reporting-user, authSource=admin):
- appdb.reports countDocuments -> 4 (readable, dashboards restored)
- appdb.raw find -> Unauthorized (raw ingestion data remains protected)
