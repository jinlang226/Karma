The routing is governed by two Ingresses on `canary.example.com`:

- **`canary-stable`** — the main Ingress, routes `/` → `stable-echo`.
- **`canary-canary`** — a canary overlay with:
  - `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/canary: "true"`
  - `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/canary-by-header: X-Canary`

This is exactly the correct header-based canary configuration: only requests with `X-Canary: always` divert to `canary-echo`; everything else falls through to the stable backend.
