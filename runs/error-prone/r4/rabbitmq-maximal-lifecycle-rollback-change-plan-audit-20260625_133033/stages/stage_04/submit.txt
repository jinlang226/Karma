**Rollback script (`rollback.sh`) is a 7-step ordered teardown:**
1. Clear `ha-all` policy from `/app`
2. Delete `app-queue` from `/app`
3. Delete `ops-queue` from `/ops`
4. Clear `app-user` permissions on `/app`
5. Clear `ops-user` permissions on `/ops`
6. Delete users `app-user` and `ops-user`
7. Delete vhosts `/app` and `/ops`

The ordering matters — queues and permissions are removed before their vhosts are deleted, and users are cleared after their permissions are gone. The `admin` user and default `/` vhost are left untouched.

The **`rollback-rehearsal` ConfigMap is created**, the cluster configuration is **verified unchanged** (all three vhosts, all three users, and the `ha-all` policy are still present).
