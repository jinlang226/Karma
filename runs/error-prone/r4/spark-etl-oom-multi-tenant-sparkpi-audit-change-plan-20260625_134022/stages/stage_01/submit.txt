## Diagnosis & Fix Summary

### Root Cause
**Data skew in Stage 3 GROUP BY user_id:**
- 100,000 total records across 1,000 distinct user IDs
- user_id=999 holds **50,000 records (50%)** — a **500x skew ratio**
- With only 256MB executor memory, the single executor handling user_id=999's partition triggered `OutOfMemoryError`

### Fix Applied (combined Option B + C)
Updated `etl-job.yaml` with two changes:
1. **Executor memory raised to 1g** (`spark.executor.memory=1g`) — gives each executor enough headroom
2. **AQE with skewJoin enabled** (`spark.sql.adaptive.enabled=true` + `spark.sql.adaptive.skewJoin.enabled=true`) — Spark automatically detects and splits oversized partitions at runtime, preventing hot-key overload

- Job status: **Complete (1/1)** in 85 seconds
- All 500 tasks finished; log ends with `spark_etl_pipeline_verified`
- No OOM errors
