All requirements are met. Here's what was done:

**Root cause:** The `prometheus-config` ConfigMap only had a self-scrape job (`localhost:9090`) — no RabbitMQ job at all.

**Fix:** Updated the ConfigMap to add a `rabbitmq` scrape job using the headless service DNS names for pod-stable addressing:

```yaml
- job_name: "rabbitmq"
  metrics_path: /metrics
  static_configs:
    - targets:
        - "rabbitmq-0.rabbitmq-headless:15692"
        - "rabbitmq-1.rabbitmq-headless:15692"
        - "rabbitmq-2.rabbitmq-headless:15692"
```

Then waited for the kubelet to sync the volume (~60s) and triggered a hot reload via `POST /-/reload` (enabled by `--web.enable-lifecycle`).

**Result:**
- 3 RabbitMQ pods: all `1/1 Running`
- 3 Prometheus targets with `job=rabbitmq`: all `up`
- `up{job="rabbitmq"}` query: 3 samples, all value=1
