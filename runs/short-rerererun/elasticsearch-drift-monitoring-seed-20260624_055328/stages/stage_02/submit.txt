3. **`filebeat-config` ConfigMap** — Filebeat 7.17.9 reading the `*_server.json` log files from the shared `es-logs` volume, shipping to the monitoring cluster.

4. **StatefulSet patch** — added:
   - `es-logs` emptyDir volume (shared between ES and Filebeat)
   - Log4j2 config override mounted in ES container
   - `metricbeat` sidecar (with cert mount + password env)
   - `filebeat` sidecar (with shared log volume)
