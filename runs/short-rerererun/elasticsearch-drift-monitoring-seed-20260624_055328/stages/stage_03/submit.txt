All three verification criteria pass:

2. **All nodes in `/_cat/nodes`** — es-cluster-0, es-cluster-1, es-cluster-2 all present

**What was wrong and what was fixed:**

The precondition deliberately corrupted the `es-config` ConfigMap by replacing the correct short-form seed_hosts (`es-cluster-0.es-cluster`) with fully-qualified but wrong-namespace addresses (`es-cluster-0.es-cluster.default.svc.cluster.local`). Since the cluster lives in the `elasticsearch` namespace, those FQDNs didn't resolve — so when es-cluster-2 was restarted with a fresh PVC, it couldn't discover the other nodes.

The fix was a one-step ConfigMap patch restoring the correct short-form DNS names (`es-cluster-{0,1,2}.es-cluster`), followed by deleting es-cluster-2 so it restarted and loaded the corrected config. Within ~60 seconds it joined as the third node and the cluster went green.
