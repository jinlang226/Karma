Both routes work correctly:

- Without header → **`stable`**
- With `X-Canary: always` → **`canary`**

**Root cause:** The `canary-canary` ingress annotations had been tampered with — `canary-by-header` was changed from `X-Canary` to `X-Feature` and `canary-by-header-value` from `always` to `v2`. Patching the annotations back to the original values restored correct header-based canary routing.
