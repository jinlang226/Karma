Both routes work correctly:

- Without `X-Feature` header → `stable`
- With `X-Feature: v2` → `canary`

**Root cause:** The `canary-canary` Ingress had `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/canary-by-header: X-Canary` and `canary-by-header-value: beta` instead of `X-Feature` / `v2`. Patching those two annotations was all that was needed — no changes to Deployments or Services.
