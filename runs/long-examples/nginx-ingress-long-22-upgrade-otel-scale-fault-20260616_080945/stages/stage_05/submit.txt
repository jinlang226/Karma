**What was done:**

2. **Created two separate Ingress resources** for `rate.example.com`:
   - `rate-api`: routes `/api` → `stable-proxy:80`, annotated with `limit-rps: "1"` and `limit-burst-multiplier: "1"` (burst=1, nodelay, 1 req/s steady state)
   - `rate-health`: routes `/health` → `stable-proxy:80`, no rate limiting

The initial attempt used `stable-echo` as the backend, which conflicted with the pre-existing `canary-alt` decoy Ingress (registered as an orphaned canary alternative backend), preventing nginx-ingress from generating the location blocks. Switching to `stable-proxy` resolved the conflict.
