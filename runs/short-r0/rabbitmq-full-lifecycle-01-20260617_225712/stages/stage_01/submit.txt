## Root cause
The `app-producer` was CrashLooping with `not_authorised` because the `app-user` had an **empty `configure` permission** on the `/app` vhost. Declaring a queue (the producer's first action via the management API) requires `configure` permission on the queue name — it had only `write` and `read`.

## Fix
Granted `app-user` full configure/write/read on `/app` (non-destructive, no data loss):
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```
Then restarted the producer deployment to break the crash loop.
