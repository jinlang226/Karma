**Root cause:** The `es-config` ConfigMap had two injected faults:
- `node.name: ` (null value) — caused Elasticsearch to crash immediately on startup before forming any connection
- `discovery.seed_hosts` pointed to `.default.svc.cluster.local` instead of `.elasticsearch.svc.cluster.local` — would have prevented cluster discovery even if the node started

**Fix:** Patched the ConfigMap to remove the null `node.name` line (Elasticsearch defaults to the pod hostname, which matches `cluster.initial_master_nodes`) and corrected the seed host namespace, then deleted the failed pod to let the StatefulSet restart it with the corrected config.
