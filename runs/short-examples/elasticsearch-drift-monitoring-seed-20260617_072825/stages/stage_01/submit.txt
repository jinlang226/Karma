The 3-node Elasticsearch cluster is fully deployed and operational. Here's what was created:

- **ConfigMap `es-config`** — `elasticsearch.yml` with cluster settings, TLS paths, and `${NODE_NAME}` interpolation for per-pod identity
- **StatefulSet `es-nodes`** (3/3 Ready) — `Parallel` pod management policy so all 3 pods start simultaneously (avoiding the chicken-and-egg deadlock where OrderedReady would block pod-1 waiting for pod-0's readiness, which requires pod-1 to exist)
- **Headless Service `es-nodes`** with `publishNotReadyAddresses: true` — makes pod DNS (`es-nodes-N.es-nodes.elasticsearch.svc`) resolve before pods are ready (needed for initial cluster discovery)
- **Service `es-http`** — ClusterIP on port 9200 for HTTPS client access
- **PDB `es-pdb`** — `maxUnavailable: 1` to protect quorum

Cluster is **green**, 3 data nodes, reads and writes succeed through `es-http` at `https://es-http.elasticsearch.svc.cluster.local:9200`.
