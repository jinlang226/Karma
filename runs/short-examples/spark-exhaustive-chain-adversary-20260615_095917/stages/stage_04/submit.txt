**Summary of diagnosis and fix:**

**Root cause — severe data skew:**
- `user_id 999` holds 50,000 out of 100,000 records (50% of all data)
- Skew ratio: **500x** (far above the 10x warning threshold)
- Stage 3's `GROUP BY user_id` funnels all 50K records for user 999 into a single executor partition, exhausting the 256MB executor memory

**Fix applied — dual approach in `etl-job.yaml`:**
1. **Executor memory raised:** `spark.executor.memory=1g` (4× the failing 256m)
2. **AQE skew handling enabled:**
   - `spark.sql.adaptive.enabled=true`
   - `spark.sql.adaptive.skewJoin.enabled=true` — automatically splits skewed partitions at runtime, so no single executor absorbs the hot key

**Result:** Job status `Complete 1/1` in 50 seconds, `spark_etl_pipeline_verified` logged at exit.
