All goals are met:

- **`app-producer`** is `1/1 Running` and actively publishing messages.
- **`/app` vhost** contains `app-queue` of type `classic` with 10 messages (and growing), durable.
- **3-node RabbitMQ cluster** (`rabbitmq-0/1/2`) is healthy with no restarts.

**Root cause:** `app-user` had `configure` permission left blank on the `/app` vhost. Declaring a queue requires configure access; without it, the management API returned `Access refused`. The fix was a single `rabbitmqctl set_permissions` call granting `app-user` `".*" ".*" ".*"` (configure/write/read) on `/app`.
