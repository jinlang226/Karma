**Root cause**: The StatefulSet command contained `if [ "$POD_NAME" = "crdb-cluster-1" ]; then HTTP_ADDR="127.0.0.1:8080"`, binding crdb-cluster-1's HTTP server to localhost only. Kubernetes health probes connect from outside the pod's network namespace, so they couldn't reach port 8080 — causing liveness probe failures, repeated pod kills, and quorum disruption.

**Fix applied**: Patched the StatefulSet to use `--http-addr=0.0.0.0:8080` unconditionally for all nodes, and restored the probe timeouts (5s) and failure thresholds (6/4) to tolerable values. Deleted the broken crdb-cluster-1 pod to force immediate recreation with the fixed template.
