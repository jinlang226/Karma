Everything is working. Here's a summary of what was done and the final state:

---

**Root Cause**: Two problems needed fixing:

1. **Monitoring ES not yet up** — The `monitoring-es` deployment started ~2 minutes after the ES cluster sidecars, so metricbeat/filebeat were failing with `connection refused`. This self-resolved as the monitoring ES became ready.

2. **ES only logged to stdout** — The ES Docker image's `log4j2.properties` uses Console-only appenders. Even though `path.logs: /var/log/es` was set in `elasticsearch.yml`, no log files were written, so filebeat had nothing to harvest.

**Fix applied**:
- Created ConfigMap `es-log4j2-config` with a modified `log4j2.properties` that adds a `RollingFile` appender writing to `${es.logs.base_path}/${es.logs.cluster_name}.log` alongside the existing Console appender.
- Patched the StatefulSet to mount this at `/usr/share/elasticsearch/config/log4j2.properties`.
- Performed a rolling restart (2/3 nodes always up; cluster remained green throughout).

**Final state**:
- All 3 ES pods: `3/3 Running` (elasticsearch + metricbeat + filebeat)
