All goals met. Here's a summary of what was wrong and what was fixed:

**Root cause — two bugs in the `rabbitmq-setup` job:**

1. **Missing configure permission**: The setup job set `"configure":""` (empty string = no configure access) for `app-user` on the `/app` vhost. Queue declaration is a _configure_ operation, so every `PUT /api/queues/%2Fapp/app-queue` returned `403 Access refused`. Fixed by setting `"configure":".*"`.

2. **`force-quorum` policy** (defense-in-depth): The setup job also pushed a policy forcing all queues to `queue-type: quorum` on both `/` and `/app`. The producer declares `x-queue-type: classic`, which would conflict with that policy once the permission was fixed. Both policies were deleted (they were already absent in this run, but the fix handles them).
