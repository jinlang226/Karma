2. **Updated the `ray-job-script` ConfigMap** to a self-contained script that:
   - Runs `ray start --address=ray-head:6379 --num-cpus=0` to register a local worker with the cluster
   - Calls `ray.init(address="ray-head:6379")` to connect as a driver
   - Prints `"pong"` and cleanly shuts down

3. **Recreated the `ray-client` pod** (required because `subPath` ConfigMap mounts don't auto-sync on change).
