The replica set is fully healthy: **1 PRIMARY** (`mongodb-replica-1`) and **2 SECONDARY** members (`mongodb-replica-0`, `mongodb-replica-2`), all 3 pods Ready.

**Root cause and fixes applied:**

1. **ConfigMap `health-check-script`** — removed `sleep 2` from `probe.sh`. The script was sleeping for 2 seconds, but both probes had `timeoutSeconds: 1`, guaranteeing immediate timeout on every check.

2. **StatefulSet probe settings** — restored the intended values that had been overwritten:
   - Liveness: `timeoutSeconds 1→5`, `failureThreshold 2→10`, `initialDelaySeconds 15→120`
   - Readiness: `timeoutSeconds 1→5`, `failureThreshold 2→6`

   The combination of a 1s timeout with a `sleep 2` in the script is what caused the cascading failure — the liveness probe was killing pods before MongoDB could even become healthy.
