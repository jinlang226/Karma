All three pods are Ready and the replica set is fully healthy:

- **mongodb-replica-1**: SECONDARY (was failing — now fixed)
- **mongodb-replica-2**: SECONDARY

**Root cause:** The `health-overrides` ConfigMap had a stale entry `mongodb-replica-1: legacyHealthPass123` that caused the health check script to attempt authentication with an outdated password. The actual `health-user` credential in MongoDB is `healthpass123` (from the `health-user-password` secret). Removing the stale override let the probe use the correct password, and the pod transitioned to Ready within ~20 seconds.
