The permissions are correctly configured. Here's a summary of what was done:

**Problem:** `reporting-user` had the `rawRead` role on `appdb`, granting `find` on `appdb.raw` — the collection that must not be exposed — while having no access to `appdb.reports` where dashboards read from.

**Fix:**
1. Created a new custom role `reportsRead` in `appdb` with `find` privilege on `appdb.reports` only
2. Revoked `rawRead` from `reporting-user`
3. Granted `reportsRead` to `reporting-user`

**Verified:**
- `reporting-user` **can** read `appdb.reports` (dashboard queries work)
- `reporting-user` **cannot** read `appdb.raw` (returns `not authorized` error)
