All pods are healthy. Here's a summary of what was fixed:

**Problems found:**
- `/app` vhost: `app-user` had configure permission empty (only write/read) — client couldn't declare queues/exchanges
- `/app` vhost: `ops-user` had full permissions — cross-vhost access that shouldn't exist
- `/ops` vhost: `ops-user` had no permissions at all — ops-client couldn't connect

**Changes made (all via `rabbitmqctl` on `rabbitmq-0`):**
1. `set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — added configure to app-user on /app
2. `clear_permissions -p /app ops-user` — removed ops-user's erroneous /app access
3. `set_permissions -p /ops ops-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — granted ops-user full access on /ops

**End state:** each user is scoped to exactly its own vhost, both client deployments are `1/1 Running`, and the 3-node cluster remains healthy.
