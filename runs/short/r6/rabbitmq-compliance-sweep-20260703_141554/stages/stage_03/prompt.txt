ops-client deployments cannot connect and are crashing. The permission
grants are also not correctly scoped: a user may currently hold access on
a vhost it should have nothing to do with.

Fix the RabbitMQ user permissions so both client deployments become
healthy AND each user is scoped to only its own vhost. Use only native
Kubernetes resources or RabbitMQ CLI/API. Do not use any CRD, Operator,
or third-party controller.

- app-user has full configure/write/read permissions on /app, and NO
  permissions on /ops.
- ops-user has full configure/write/read permissions on /ops, and NO
  permissions on /app.
- app-client and ops-client deployments are running and healthy.
- The cluster remains healthy with 3 nodes.

Inspect the current per-vhost permissions (commands below) before fixing,
and remove any grant that violates the required end state.
