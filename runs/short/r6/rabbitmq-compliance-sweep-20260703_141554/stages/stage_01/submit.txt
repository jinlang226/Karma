All three goals are satisfied:

**Root cause:** `app-user` had empty `configure` permissions on the `/app` vhost. RabbitMQ requires `configure` access to declare (create) a queue — `write` alone only allows publishing to existing queues.

**Fix applied:**
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```
This granted `app-user` full configure/write/read on `/app`.

- `app-producer` — `1/1 Running`, 0 restarts, stable
