Both criteria pass:

- Without `X-Canary` header → `stable`
- With `X-Canary: always` → `canary`

**Root cause:** The `canary-canary` Ingress had `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/canary-by-header-value: wrong` — a deliberately wrong value that prevented the header match from ever triggering. Patching it to `always` makes NGINX route requests carrying `X-Canary: always` to the canary backend while all other traffic continues to the stable backend.
