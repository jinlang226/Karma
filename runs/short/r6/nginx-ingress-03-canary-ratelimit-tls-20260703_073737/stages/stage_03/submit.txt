**What was wrong:** The `demo-ingress` Ingress was referencing `expired-tls-secret`, which held a TLS certificate that expired on 2020-01-02 — over 6 years ago.

**What I did:**
1. Located the Test CA private key at `/tmp/test-ca.kc-karma-6.key` (generated by the precondition setup on the host)
2. Generated a new 10-year leaf cert for `demo.example.com` with the correct SAN, signed by the Test CA
3. Created a new secret `demo-tls-secret` with the valid cert/key
4. Patched `demo-ingress` to reference `demo-tls-secret` instead of `expired-tls-secret`

The TLS handshake now completes successfully — `SSL certificate verify ok`, cert issued by `CN=Test CA`, valid 2026–2036, and the backend returns `hello` with HTTP 200.
