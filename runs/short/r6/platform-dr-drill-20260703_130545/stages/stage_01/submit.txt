The 3-node CockroachDB cluster is fully deployed and healthy. Here's a summary of what was created:

- **Headless Service** `crdb-cluster` — ClusterIP: None, ports 26257/8080, selects on the required label pair
- **Public Service** `crdb-cluster-public` — ClusterIP, same ports and selector
- **PodDisruptionBudget** `crdb-cluster` — `minAvailable: 2` to preserve quorum during voluntary disruptions
- **StatefulSet** `crdb-cluster` — 3 replicas, `cockroachdb/cockroach:v24.1.0`, insecure mode, each pod advertises its full DNS name and joins all 3 peers, 10Gi PVC per pod

All 3 nodes show `is_available: true` and `is_live: true`. The readiness probe uses `/health` (without `?ready=1`) so pods pass the probe before `cockroach init` — the standard pattern for bootstrapping a CockroachDB cluster on Kubernetes without an operator.
