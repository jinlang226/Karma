Everything is working. Here's a summary of what was deployed:

**Resources created in `elasticsearch` namespace:**

| Resource | Name | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| ConfigMap | `es-config` | `elasticsearch.yml` — HTTP+transport TLS via `es-http-tls` secret |
| Service | `es-headless` | ClusterIP None, `publishNotReadyAddresses: true`, used for discovery |
| Service | `es-http` | ClusterIP, port 9200 HTTPS — the required endpoint |
| PodDisruptionBudget | `es-pdb` | maxUnavailable: 1 |
| StatefulSet | `es` | 3 replicas, `podManagementPolicy: Parallel`, 2Gi PVCs |

**Key decisions:**
- `podManagementPolicy: Parallel` — all 3 pods start simultaneously; without this, es-0 deadlocks waiting for es-1/es-2 to form the quorum needed for its own readiness probe to pass.
- Same `es-http-tls` certificate reused for transport SSL with `verification_mode: certificate` (validates CA chain, relaxes hostname check) — avoids needing a separate transport cert.
- `ELASTIC_PASSWORD` injected from the existing `elastic-password` secret; no password bootstrapping needed.

**Final state:** 3-node cluster green, 3/3 nodes, HTTPS reads and writes confirmed through `es-http:9200`.
