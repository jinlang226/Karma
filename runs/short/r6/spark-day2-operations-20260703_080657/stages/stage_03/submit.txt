### Key observations

- **Broadcast Join** (best): ~2.3x faster. Eliminated the shuffle entirely by broadcasting the small products table to every executor. No skew possible since there's no join shuffle.
- **AQE** (~2x faster): Spark 3.5's Adaptive Query Execution detected the skewed partitions at runtime and automatically split them. Slightly slower than broadcast because it still does a shuffle but then coalesces/splits intelligently.
