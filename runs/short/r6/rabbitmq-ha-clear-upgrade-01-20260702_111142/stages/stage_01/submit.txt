All goals are met:

**Root cause:** `app-user` had empty `configure` permissions on the `/app` vhost — write and read were granted but configure was blank. Declaring (creating) a queue requires configure permission, so the producer got `Access refused` every attempt.

**Fix applied:**
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```

- `app-producer` pod: `1/1 Running`, 0 restarts
- `/app` vhost: `app-queue` (type `classic`) exists with 34 messages and growing
