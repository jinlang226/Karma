Everything is working. Here's a summary of what was deployed:

**Resources created:**
- `Service/es-transport` (headless, with `publishNotReadyAddresses: true`) — inter-node DNS and transport discovery
- `Service/es-http` — HTTPS client service on port 9200
- `ConfigMap/es-config` — Elasticsearch config with TLS on both HTTP and transport layers, seeding all 3 node FQDNs
- `StatefulSet/es-transport` (3 replicas, `podManagementPolicy: Parallel`) — 3 Elasticsearch 8.11.1 nodes

**Key fixes needed during deployment:**
1. `podManagementPolicy: Parallel` was required — with the default `OrderedReady`, pod-0 could never become ready because it needs nodes 1 & 2 to form a quorum, but they wouldn't start until pod-0 was ready (circular deadlock).
2. `publishNotReadyAddresses: true` on the headless service — without it, Kubernetes withholds DNS records for unready pods, preventing the nodes from discovering each other to form the cluster.
