## Upgrade Summary: RabbitMQ 3.9 → 4.1

**Path taken:** `3.9.29 → 3.13.7 → 4.1.8` (two-step skip-upgrade)

**Why two steps instead of direct:** RabbitMQ 4.x nodes require 3.13-era feature flags to be enabled before joining. The 3.9→3.13 jump also hit an Erlang OTP 25→26 distribution incompatibility that prevented automatic cluster re-join during the rolling update, requiring manual `rabbitmqctl join_cluster` recovery at each step.

**What was done:**
1. Set HA-all mirror policy on `/app` vhost to protect messages during restarts
2. Updated ConfigMap: replaced deprecated `queue_master_locator` with `queue_leader_locator` (required for 4.x compatibility)
3. Patched StatefulSet to `rabbitmq:3.13-management` — nodes came up as separate single-node clusters (Erlang 25↔26 OTP issue), manually joined them with `rabbitmqctl join_cluster`
4. Recreated `/app` vhost, `app-user` (from secret), granted permissions and management tag
5. Enabled ALL feature flags on 3.13 (prerequisite for 4.x join)
6. Patched StatefulSet to `rabbitmq:4.1-management` — rolling upgrade worked (both use Erlang 27 now), only `rabbitmq-0` needed manual cluster join
7. Enabled `rabbitmq_4.0.0` and `rabbitmq_4.1.0` feature flags to complete the 4.x migration

**Final state:**
- All 3 nodes: **RabbitMQ 4.1.8 on Erlang 27.3.4.13** ✅
- Cluster: 3 nodes running, no alarms, no partitions ✅
- `/app` vhost exists ✅
- `app-queue`: classic, durable, **79 messages**, running ✅ (producer continuously publishing)
