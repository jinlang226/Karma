All three pods are `1/1 Ready` and the replica set has exactly 1 PRIMARY (`mongodb-replica-2`) and 2 SECONDARYs (`mongodb-replica-0`, `mongodb-replica-1`).

**Root cause and fix:**

The probes were misconfigured with `timeoutSeconds: 1`, but `mongosh` takes ~3 seconds to start up and execute — so every probe invocation timed out, hitting the `failureThreshold: 2` limit and causing repeated pod restarts.

**Changes made to the `mongodb-replica` StatefulSet probes (both liveness and readiness):**

| Setting | Before | After |
|---|---|---|
| `timeoutSeconds` | 1s | **5s** |
| `initialDelaySeconds` | 5s (readiness) / 15s (liveness) | **30s** |
| `failureThreshold` | 2 | **3** |
