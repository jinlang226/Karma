Everything is healthy and working. Here's a summary of what was deployed:

**Replica Set:** `mongodb-replica` — 1 PRIMARY (`mongodb-replica-0`) + 2 SECONDARY (`mongodb-replica-1`, `mongodb-replica-2`), all health=1.

**Resources in `mongodb` namespace:**
- `StatefulSet/mongodb-replica` — 3/3 pods running, backed by `mongo:6.0` with keyfile auth
- `Service/mongodb-replica-svc` — headless (`clusterIP: None`), port 27017; each pod reachable at `mongodb-replica-<n>.mongodb-replica-svc.mongodb.svc.cluster.local`
- `Secret/mongodb-keyfile` — shared keyfile for internal RS auth
- `Secret/admin-user-password` — key `password: admin123password`
- `Secret/app-user-password` — key `password: app123password`

**Users:** `admin-user` (root on admin), `app-user` (readWrite on appdb). Data write and read verified via `app-user`.

**Root cause fixed in entrypoint script:** `rs.initiate()` was called before DNS entries for pods 1 and 2 were registered (they start in parallel), causing the quorum check to fail silently. The updated ConfigMap retries `rs.initiate()` in a loop until DNS resolves, so future pod restarts will initialize correctly.
