Everything is working. Here's what was done:

**Root cause:** The `prometheus-config` ConfigMap's `prometheus.yml` only had a `prometheus` self-scrape job. There was no `rabbitmq` job configured.

**Fix:** Patched the ConfigMap to add:
1. A `rabbitmq` scrape job targeting all 3 pods via their individual headless-service DNS names (`rabbitmq-{0,1,2}.rabbitmq-headless.<namespace>.svc.cluster.local:15692`), which gives 3 distinct targets instead of load-balancing through the ClusterIP service.
2. A `rule_files` entry pointing to `/etc/prometheus/rules.yml` (the recording rule `rabbitmq_up = up{job="rabbitmq"}` was already in the ConfigMap but not being loaded).

Then triggered a `POST /-/reload` twice (the first reload raced the ConfigMap file propagation; the second picked up the complete file).

**Final state:**
- 4 Prometheus targets UP (prometheus self + 3 RabbitMQ pods)
- `rabbitmq_up` returns **3 samples**, all = 1
- All 3 RabbitMQ pods Running, no restarts
