Everything is in the required end state. Here's a summary of what was fixed:

**Problems found:**
1. `/app` vhost — `app-user` had empty configure permission (`""`) instead of `.*`
2. `/app` vhost — `ops-user` had full permissions on `/app` (wrong vhost, should be scoped to `/ops` only)
3. `/ops` vhost — `ops-user` had no permissions at all
4. `app-queue` had been pre-created with `x-queue-mode=lazy` argument, conflicting with how `app-client` tries to declare it (causing an "inequivalent arg" error even after permissions were fixed)

**Fixes applied:**
1. `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — restored full configure/write/read for app-user on /app
2. `rabbitmqctl clear_permissions -p /app ops-user` — removed ops-user's cross-vhost access
3. `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /ops ops-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — granted ops-user full access on its correct vhost
4. `rabbitmqctl delete_queue app-queue -p /app` — removed the mismatched queue so app-client could recreate it with correct arguments

**End state:**
- `app-user`: full `.*`/`.*`/`.*` on `/app` only
- `ops-user`: full `.*`/`.*`/`.*` on `/ops` only
- `app-client` and `ops-client`: both `1/1 Running`
- 3-node RabbitMQ cluster: all nodes running
