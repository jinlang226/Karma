All goals are met:

**Root cause:** `app-user` had empty `configure` permission on the `/app` vhost — the configure permission controls the ability to declare (create) queues and exchanges. Without it, every attempt to declare `app-queue` was refused with `Access refused`.

**Fix:** Granted `app-user` full `configure`, `write`, and `read` permissions on `/app`:
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```

- `app-producer` — `1/1 Running`, no more crashes
- `/app` vhost — `app-queue` (classic) with 6 messages and counting
