All goals are met:

**Root cause:** `app-user` had empty `configure` permission on the `/app` vhost. Declaring (creating) a queue requires configure permission matching the queue name — so every attempt to declare `app-queue` was rejected with "Access refused."

**Fix applied:**
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```
This grants `app-user` full configure/write/read on `/app`.

**Verified state:**
- `app-producer` is `1/1 Ready` (no more CrashLoop)
- `/app` vhost contains `app-queue` of type `classic` with 26+ messages
- All 3 RabbitMQ nodes (`rabbitmq-0/1/2`) are running and clustered
