The 3-node CockroachDB cluster is fully deployed and healthy. Here's a summary of what was deployed and one gotcha resolved:

**Resources created:**
- `Service/crdb-cluster` — headless service (DNS for inter-node gossip) with `publishNotReadyAddresses: true`
- `Service/crdb-cluster-public` — ClusterIP service for SQL/HTTP client access
- `StatefulSet/crdb-cluster` — 3 replicas, `podManagementPolicy: Parallel`, image `cockroachdb/cockroach:v24.1.0`, 10Gi PVC per pod
- `PodDisruptionBudget/crdb-cluster-pdb` — `minAvailable: 2` (quorum preserved during voluntary disruptions)

**Labels:** all pods, services, and the StatefulSet carry `app.kubernetes.io/name: cockroachdb` + `app.kubernetes.io/instance: crdb-cluster`.

**Two bootstrapping issues resolved:**
1. `podManagementPolicy: Parallel` — required because with default `OrderedReady`, pod-1 waits for pod-0 to be Ready, but pod-0 can't be Ready without pod-1 present to form a quorum. Parallel starts all 3 simultaneously, then `cockroach init` is run once.
2. `publishNotReadyAddresses: true` on the headless service — required so DNS entries (`crdb-cluster-{0,1,2}.crdb-cluster.cockroachdb`) are resolvable before pods pass readiness. Without this, gossip DNS lookups fail and nodes can never join.
