The 3-node CockroachDB cluster is fully deployed and healthy. Summary of what was created:

- **Service `crdb-cluster`** — headless (ClusterIP: None), exposing grpc/26257 and http/8080, selecting on both required labels
- **Service `crdb-cluster-public`** — standard ClusterIP service on the same ports
- **StatefulSet `crdb-cluster`** — 3 replicas, image `cockroachdb/cockroach:v24.1.0`, `podManagementPolicy: Parallel` (needed so all 3 nodes start simultaneously before init), 10Gi PVC per pod
- **PodDisruptionBudget** — `minAvailable: 2` to preserve quorum during voluntary disruptions
- All pods carry labels `app.kubernetes.io/name: cockroachdb` and `app.kubernetes.io/instance: crdb-cluster`

The key issue resolved: StatefulSet's default sequential pod creation caused a deadlock (pod 0 needed the other nodes to pass `health?ready=1`), fixed by switching to `Parallel` pod management and running `cockroach init` once all nodes were up.
