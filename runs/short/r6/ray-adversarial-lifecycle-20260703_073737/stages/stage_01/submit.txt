**One wrinkle:** `ray.init(address='ray-head:6379')` in Ray 2.9 doesn't work as a pure client call — it tries to start a local raylet in the calling pod and waits up to 60 s for `node_ip_address.json` to appear. The first attempt timed out because the local raylet in the `ray-client` pod didn't initialize in time. Running `ray start --address=ray-head:6379 --num-cpus=0` in the pod first established the session; subsequent `ray.init()` calls reuse that session and connect immediately.
