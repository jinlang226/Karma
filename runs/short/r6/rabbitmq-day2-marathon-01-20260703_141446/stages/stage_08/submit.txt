Everything is working. Here's what was done and why:

**Root cause:** The `prometheus-config` ConfigMap only contained a self-scrape job (`localhost:9090`) with no RabbitMQ scrape job at all.

**Fix:** Patched the ConfigMap to add a `rabbitmq` scrape job targeting all 3 pods by their stable headless-service DNS names (`rabbitmq-{0,1,2}.rabbitmq-headless:15692`), then restarted the Prometheus deployment to immediately mount the updated ConfigMap (rather than waiting ~60s for Kubernetes to sync the volume).

**Verification:**
- `up{job="rabbitmq"}` returns **3 samples, all value 1**
- All 3 RabbitMQ pods remain `Running 1/1` with 0 restarts
