All done. Here's what was wrong and what was fixed:

**Root cause:** The StatefulSet probes had been tuned to values that made them fail before mongosh could complete:
- `timeoutSeconds: 1` — mongosh with auth takes longer than 1 second (confirmed by the `command timed out after 1s` events)
- `initialDelaySeconds: 5` (readiness) / `15` (liveness) — not enough time for MongoDB to start
- `failureThreshold: 2` — no tolerance for the startup window

**Fix:** Restored probe values to the original working configuration:

| Probe | Before | After |
|---|---|---|
| Readiness `initialDelaySeconds` | 5 | 30 |
| Readiness `timeoutSeconds` | 1 | 5 |
| Readiness `failureThreshold` | 2 | 6 |
| Liveness `initialDelaySeconds` | 15 | 60 |
| Liveness `timeoutSeconds` | 1 | 5 |
| Liveness `failureThreshold` | 2 | 10 |

**Result:** All 3 pods are `1/1 Ready` with 1 PRIMARY (replica-2) and 2 SECONDARY members. Authentication remains enabled.
