All goals met. Here's a summary of what was wrong and what was fixed:

**Root cause:** `app-user` had empty `configure` permission on the `/app` vhost (`"" ".*" ".*"` — write and read only). RabbitMQ requires `configure` permission to declare (create) queues, so every attempt to declare `app-queue` returned `Access refused`.

**Fix:** Granted `app-user` full permissions on `/app`:
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```

**Verification:**
- `app-producer` is `1/1 Running` with no restarts
- `app-queue` (type: `classic`) exists in `/app` vhost with 13 messages and counting
- All 3 RabbitMQ nodes remain healthy
