Everything is working. Here's a summary of what was wrong and what was fixed:

**Root cause:** `app-user` was missing the `configure` permission on the `/app` vhost. The RabbitMQ Management API requires `configure` access to declare (create) a queue — `app-user` only had `write` and `read`, so the PUT request to declare `app-queue` returned `Access refused`.

**Fix:** Granted `app-user` full `configure`, `write`, `read` permissions on `/app`:
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```
