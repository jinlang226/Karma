**Root cause:** `app-user` had permissions set on the `/app` vhost with an empty `configure` regex (`write`/`read` were `.*` but `configure` was blank), so RabbitMQ refused the queue declaration with `not_authorised`. Fixed by running `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — a metadata-only change with no data loss, no CRDs/operators involved.
