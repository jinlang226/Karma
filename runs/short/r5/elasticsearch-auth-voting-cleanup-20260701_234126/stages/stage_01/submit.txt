**Resources created in `elasticsearch` namespace:**

| Resource | Name | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| ConfigMap | `es-config` | `elasticsearch.yml` with TLS + security config |
| Service (headless) | `es-headless` | Inter-node discovery DNS (`publishNotReadyAddresses: true`) |
| Service (ClusterIP) | `es-http` | HTTPS access on port 9200 |
| StatefulSet | `elasticsearch` | 3-replica cluster |

**Key design decisions:**
- `discovery.seed_hosts` uses full pod FQDNs (`elasticsearch-{0,1,2}.es-headless.elasticsearch.svc.cluster.local`) to avoid the chicken-and-egg DNS failure when no pods are ready yet
- `publishNotReadyAddresses: true` on the headless service ensures transport-layer peer discovery works during startup
- The provided `es-http-tls` secret is mounted and used for both HTTP and transport TLS (with `verification_mode: certificate` to allow hostname flexibility)
- `ELASTIC_PASSWORD` is injected from the `elastic-password` secret
- An init container sets `vm.max_map_count=262144` as required by Elasticsearch
