**Summary of what happened and what I did:**

The binary upgrade (rolling restart to v24.1.0) and cluster finalization had already occurred, but the adversary had throttled `kv.snapshot_rebalance.max_rate` from the default **32 MiB** down to **1 MiB**. This severely slowed range rebalancing after node `crdb-cluster-0` restarted last in the rolling upgrade, causing:
- Node liveness heartbeat failures (Raft commits taking 17–27s)
- 46 under-replicated ranges
- All nodes oscillating between live/not-live

**Fix applied:** Reset `kv.snapshot_rebalance.max_rate` to the default (`RESET CLUSTER SETTING kv.snapshot_rebalance.max_rate` → 32 MiB). This allowed rebalancing to proceed at the correct rate, ranges recovered from 46 → 0 under-replicated over ~35 minutes, and all three nodes are now fully healthy.
