Everything is healthy. Here's what was wrong and what I did:

**Root cause:** `app-user` had write and read permissions on the `/app` vhost but an empty **configure** permission. In RabbitMQ, `configure` permission is required to declare (create) queues and exchanges. Without it, the producer got `Access refused` when calling `queue.declare`.

**Fix:** Granted `app-user` full configure/write/read on `/app`:
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```

**Verified state:**
- `app-producer` is `1/1 Running` (no restarts after the fix)
- `/app` vhost contains `app-queue` of type `classic` with messages (7 messages accumulated)
- All 3 RabbitMQ nodes (`rabbitmq-0/1/2`) remain `Running`
