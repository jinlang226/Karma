All goals are met. Here's the summary of what was wrong and what was fixed:

**Root cause:** `app-user` had write and read permissions on the `/app` vhost, but the **configure** permission was empty (`""`). RabbitMQ requires configure permission matching the queue name to declare (create) a queue. Without it, the AMQP `queue.declare` call returned `Access refused`.

**Fix:** Granted full permissions to `app-user` on `/app`:
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```

- `app-producer` is `1/1 Running` (0 restarts since fix)
- `/app` vhost contains `app-queue` of type **classic** with **50 messages**
- All 3 RabbitMQ nodes (`rabbitmq-0/1/2`) are running, no alarms, no network partitions
