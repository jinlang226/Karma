All requirements are met. Here's a summary of what was found and fixed:

**Problems found:**
1. `/app` vhost — `app-user` had empty configure permission (`""`) but needed `".*"` (blocking queue declaration)
2. `/app` vhost — `ops-user` had full permissions there (wrong vhost entirely)
3. `/ops` vhost — `ops-user` had zero permissions (couldn't access the vhost at all)
4. A secondary issue: `app-queue` in `/app` had been pre-created with `x-queue-mode=lazy`, causing an argument mismatch when `app-client` tried to re-declare it — deleted the queue so the client could create it correctly

- `clear_permissions -p /app ops-user` — removed ops-user's cross-vhost access to /app
- `set_permissions -p /ops ops-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — granted ops-user full permissions on /ops
- `delete_queue app-queue -p /app` — cleared the mismatched pre-existing queue
