All goals are met:

- **app-producer** is `1/1 Running` (Ready).
- **`/app` vhost** has `app-queue`, type `classic`, with messages accumulating (6 and growing, since the producer publishes on a loop).
- **Cluster** shows all 3 nodes (`rabbitmq-0/1/2`) running and healthy.

Root cause: `app-user`'s permissions on the `/app` vhost had an empty `configure` regex, so it was denied when trying to declare (`PUT`) `app-queue`, causing the producer to crash-loop with `not_authorised`. Fix was a single `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user "^app-queue$" ".*" ".*"` — scoped narrowly to just `app-queue` rather than granting broad configure rights, no CRDs/operators used, no data touched (no data loss risk).
