All three goals are met:

1. **`app-producer` is `1/1 Running`** — no more CrashLoop.
2. **`/app` vhost has `app-queue` of type `classic` with messages** (16 and growing as the producer keeps publishing).
3. **All 3 RabbitMQ nodes remain healthy** (`rabbitmq-0/1/2` all `1/1 Running`).

**Root cause:** `app-user` had write/read permissions on `/app` but an empty configure permission (`""`). Declaring (creating) a queue requires configure permission. The fix was:
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```
