The CockroachDB cluster is fully deployed and healthy. Here's a summary of what was created:

**Resources deployed:**
- **Headless Service** `crdb-cluster` — ClusterIP: None, exposes grpc/26257 and http/8080, used for pod DNS resolution
- **Public Service** `crdb-cluster-public` — ClusterIP: 10.96.99.29, exposes same ports for client access
- **StatefulSet** `crdb-cluster` — 3/3 ready, `podManagementPolicy: Parallel` (required so all pods start simultaneously before init)
- **PVCs** — 3×10Gi volumes, all Bound
