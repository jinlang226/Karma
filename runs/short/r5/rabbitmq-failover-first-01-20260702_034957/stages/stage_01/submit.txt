All three goals are met:

- **`app-producer` is Ready** — `1/1 Running`, no longer crashing.
- **`/app` vhost has `app-queue`** — classic type, 9 messages (and counting every 2 seconds).
- **3-node cluster is healthy** — all three nodes (`rabbitmq-0/1/2`) are running and joined.

**Root cause:** `app-user` had empty `configure` permission on the `/app` vhost (`""` instead of `".*"`). Declaring a queue is a *configure* operation in RabbitMQ, so the user was refused even though write/read were granted. The fix was a single `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` to restore full configure access.
