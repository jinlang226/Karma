All goals confirmed:

- **Root cause**: `app-user`'s permissions on the `/app` vhost had an empty `configure` pattern (`write`/`read` were `.*` but `configure` was blank), so the producer's `PUT /api/queues/%2Fapp/app-queue` (which requires `configure`) failed with `not_authorised`.
- **Fix**: `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — no data loss, no CRDs/operators involved.
- **Verification**: `app-producer` pod is `1/1 Running`, `app-queue` exists in `/app` as type `classic` and is accumulating messages (8 and growing), and the 3-node cluster is healthy (`rabbitmq-0/1/2` all Running Nodes).
