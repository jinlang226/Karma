- `set_permissions -p /ops ops-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — full configure/write/read for ops-user on `/ops`
- confirmed app-user has no grant on `/ops` (already absent)

**Verified end state:**
- `/app`: only `admin` and `app-user` (full perms) — `ops-user` removed
