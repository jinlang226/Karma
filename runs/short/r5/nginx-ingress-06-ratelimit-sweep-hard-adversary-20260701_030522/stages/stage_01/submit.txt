**Changes made:** Added two annotations to the `rate-api` Ingress in the `demo` namespace:
- `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/limit-rps: "2"` — 2 requests/second per replica
- `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/limit-burst-multiplier: "1"` — burst = rps × multiplier = 2 tokens per replica

**Why this works with 3 replicas:** The key challenge is that each nginx replica maintains its own independent rate-limit counter. With 3 replicas:
- Sustained effective rate across all replicas: 3 × 2 = **6 RPS** (≈ the target 5 RPS)
- Maximum burst capacity: 3 × 2 = **6 tokens total**
- For a 20-request burst: at most ~6–7 requests pass, leaving ≥13 rejected (well above the oracle's minimum of 4)
