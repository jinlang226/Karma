All goals are met:

- **Root cause:** `app-user` had `write` and `read` permissions on the `/app` vhost but the `configure` permission was empty — so the `PUT /api/queues/%2Fapp/app-queue` call to declare the queue was rejected with `Access refused`.
- **Fix:** Ran `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` to grant full configure/write/read access.
- **Result:**
  - `app-producer` is `1/1 Running` (Ready)
  - `/app` vhost contains `app-queue` of type `classic` with 6 messages (and accumulating)
  - All 3 RabbitMQ nodes remain healthy (`rabbitmq-0/1/2` all `Running`)
