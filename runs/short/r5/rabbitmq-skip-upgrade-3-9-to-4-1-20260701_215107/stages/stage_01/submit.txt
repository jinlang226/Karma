All goals are met:

**Root cause:** `app-user` had write and read permissions on the `/app` vhost but the **configure** permission was empty. RabbitMQ requires configure permission to declare (create) a queue, so every attempt by `app-producer` to create `app-queue` was refused with `Access refused`.

**Fix:** Granted `app-user` full `.*` configure permission on `/app` via `rabbitmqctl set_permissions`.

- `app-producer` is `1/1 Running`
- `/app` vhost contains `app-queue` of type `classic` with 16 messages (and growing)
- 3-node cluster is healthy with all nodes running
