All goals are met:

**Root cause:** `app-user` was missing the `configure` permission on the `/app` vhost (`"" ".*" ".*"`). RabbitMQ requires `configure` permission to declare (create) a queue — without it the broker returns `access_refused`.

**Fix:** `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — grants configure/write/read on all resources in `/app`.

**Outcome:**
- `app-producer` is `1/1 Running` (0 restarts on new pod)
- `/app` vhost contains `app-queue` of type `classic` with messages accumulating
- All 3 RabbitMQ nodes remain `Running`, cluster healthy
