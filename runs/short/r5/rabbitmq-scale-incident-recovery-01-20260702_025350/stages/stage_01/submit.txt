**Root cause:** `app-user` had empty `configure` permissions on the `/app` vhost. RabbitMQ requires the `configure` permission to declare (create) a queue — without it, the declaration fails with `Access refused`, causing the producer to crash.

**Fix applied:** `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user '.*' '.*' '.*'` — grants `app-user` full configure/write/read on `/app`.
