All goals met:

- **app-producer** is `1/1 Running` (no restarts) and continuously publishing.
- **`/app` vhost** has `app-queue` of type `classic` with 10 messages and growing.
- **Cluster** has all 3 nodes (`rabbitmq-0/1/2`) as running disk nodes, healthy.

Root cause: `app-user` had permissions granted for `write`/`read` on `/app` but an empty `configure` regex, so the HTTP API's `PUT /api/queues/%2Fapp/app-queue` (which requires `configure` permission to declare the queue) was refused with `not_authorised`. Fixed with `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user '.*' '.*' '.*'` — no CRDs/operators used, no data touched/lost.
