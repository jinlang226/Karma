All goals are met. Here's what was broken and what was fixed:

**Root causes found:**

1. **StatefulSet scaled down to 1 replica** — an external process reduced `rabbitmq` StatefulSet from 3 → 1, causing `rabbitmq-1` and `rabbitmq-2` to terminate and the `rabbit` app on `rabbitmq-0` to stop (it was mid-shutdown when I arrived). Fixed by scaling back to 3 and starting the rabbit app.

2. **Missing configure permission for `app-user` on `/app` vhost** — the user had `write` and `read` permissions but no `configure` permission, so it couldn't declare (create) `app-queue`. Fixed with `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"`.

**Verified end state:**
- `app-producer` is `1/1 Ready` publishing messages (186 in queue)
- `/app` vhost contains `app-queue` (classic, durable, lazy) with active messages
- 3-node cluster healthy: `rabbitmq-0`, `rabbitmq-1`, `rabbitmq-2` all running RabbitMQ 3.12.14
