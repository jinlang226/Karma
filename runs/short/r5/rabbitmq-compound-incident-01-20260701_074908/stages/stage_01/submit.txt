All goals are met. Here's what was wrong and what was fixed:

**Root cause:** `app-user` was missing the **configure** permission on the `/app` vhost. The permissions were `("", ".*", ".*")` — configure was empty. Declaring a queue requires configure permission matching the queue name.

**Fix:** Granted full permissions:
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```

- `app-producer` pod: `1/1 Running`, 0 restarts
