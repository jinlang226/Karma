Everything is fixed. Here's the summary:

**Root cause:** `app-user` had write+read permissions on the `/app` vhost but was **missing configure permission** (`""`). The RabbitMQ management API requires configure permission to declare (create) queues — without it, the PUT request to `/api/queues/%2Fapp/app-queue` returned `{"error":"not_authorised","reason":"Access refused."}`.

**Fix:** One command:
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```
