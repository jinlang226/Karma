Both criteria are met:

**What I did:** Added two annotations to the existing `rate-api` Ingress only:
- `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/limit-rps: "1"` — caps `/api` at 1 request per second per client IP.
- `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/limit-burst-multiplier: "1"` — sets the burst bucket to 1×RPS (=1), so excess requests beyond the first burst slot are rejected as 503 rather than queued.

The `rate-health` Ingress was not touched, so `/health` remains unlimited.
