**Root cause:** `app-user` was missing the `configure` permission (`""`) on the `/app` vhost. Without it, the RabbitMQ HTTP API returned `Access refused` when the producer tried to declare `app-queue`.

**Fix applied:** Granted `app-user` full `configure`/`write`/`read` permissions (`.*`) on the `/app` vhost:
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```
