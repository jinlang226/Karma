**Fix — two changes to the `prometheus-config` ConfigMap:**

2. **Added a recording rule** (`rules.yml` key in the same ConfigMap, mounted automatically at `/etc/prometheus/rules.yml`):

   - record: rabbitmq_up
     expr: up{job="rabbitmq"}
