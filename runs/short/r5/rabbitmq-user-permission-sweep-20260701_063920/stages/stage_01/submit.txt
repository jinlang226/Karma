All goals are confirmed met:

- **`app-producer` is Ready** — `1/1 Running`, no restarts since the fix.
- **`/app` vhost has `app-queue`, type `classic`, with messages** — currently 9 and growing (producer publishes continuously).
- **Cluster remains healthy with 3 nodes** — `rabbitmq-0/1/2` all `Running`, all 3 listed as active cluster nodes.

Root cause: `app-user` had an empty `configure` permission regex on the `/app` vhost (write/read were `.*` but configure was blank), so the management API's `PUT /queues/%2Fapp/app-queue` call to declare the queue was refused with `not_authorised`, crash-looping the producer. Fix: `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` to grant configure rights, then restarted the producer pod. No CRDs/operators used, and no data was touched or lost — only a permission grant was applied.
