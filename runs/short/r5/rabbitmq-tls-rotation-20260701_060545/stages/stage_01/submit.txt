The old pod finished terminating on its own. Everything is now clean and stable:

**Root cause:** `app-user` (the credentials `app-producer` uses) had permissions granted on `/app` for `write`/`read` but an empty `configure` permission, so it was refused when trying to declare `app-queue` (`not_authorised`).

**Fix:** `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — restored full configure/write/read permissions for `app-user` on the `/app` vhost (no data loss, no CRDs/operators involved).

- `app-producer` pod is `1/1 Running`/Ready, no crash loop.
- `/app` vhost has `app-queue` (type `classic`) with messages accumulating (17+ and growing).
