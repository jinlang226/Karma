**Service**

**StatefulSet** — `mongodb-replica`, 3 replicas
- Pods: `mongodb-replica-0` (PRIMARY), `mongodb-replica-1` (SECONDARY), `mongodb-replica-2` (SECONDARY)
- `replSetName`: `mongodb-replica`
