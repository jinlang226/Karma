The cluster is fully operational. Here's a summary of what was wrong and how it was fixed:

**Root cause:** The StatefulSet command hardcoded `default.svc.cluster.local` in both `--advertise-host` and `--join` flags, but the pods live in the `cockroachdb` namespace. DNS resolution for `crdb-cluster-1.crdb-cluster.default.svc.cluster.local` fails because the correct domain is `crdb-cluster-1.crdb-cluster.cockroachdb.svc.cluster.local`.

**Fix:**
1. Patched the StatefulSet to replace `default` with `cockroachdb` in all three addresses.
2. Deleted all 3 pods simultaneously (forced by the rolling-update deadlock — pods can't become Ready until init, but init needs all nodes to resolve each other).
3. Ran `cockroach init --insecure` once all nodes were communicating correctly.

**Result:** 3/3 nodes healthy (`is_available=true`, `is_live=true`), SQL connections accepted.
