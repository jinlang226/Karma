All three goals are met:

- **/app vhost contains `app-queue` of type `classic` with 24 messages** (and growing)
- **3-node RabbitMQ cluster is healthy**

**Root cause:** `app-user` was missing `configure` permission in the `/app` vhost (the permission entry had an empty string for `configure`). Declaring a queue requires configure permission matching the queue name — without it, every declaration attempt returned `"Access refused."` and crashed the producer. Fix was a single `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` call.
