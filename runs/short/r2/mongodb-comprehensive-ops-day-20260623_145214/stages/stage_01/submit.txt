The replica set is fully restored. Here's what happened and what was fixed:

**Root cause:** Member 1's hostname in the replica set config pointed to a non-existent service `mongo-wrong` (`mongodb-replica-1.mongo-wrong.svc.cluster.local:27017`). The correct service is `mongodb-replica-svc`.

**Fix:** Used `rs.reconfig()` from the PRIMARY (member 0) to correct member 1's host to `mongodb-replica-1.mongodb-replica-svc.mongodb.svc.cluster.local:27017`.

**Result:**
- `mongodb-replica-0` → PRIMARY, health=1
- `mongodb-replica-1` → SECONDARY, health=1  
- `mongodb-replica-2` → SECONDARY, health=1
- All 3 writable voting members, majority quorum satisfied, no data loss (data was intact on all PVCs throughout).
