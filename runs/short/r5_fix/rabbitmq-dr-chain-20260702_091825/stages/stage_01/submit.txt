All goals are met:

- **Root cause:** `app-user` had empty `configure` permission on the `/app` vhost. Declaring a queue requires configure access (`.*`), so every attempt to create `app-queue` returned `Access refused`.
- **Fix:** `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — granted full configure/write/read on `/app`.
- **Result:**
  - `app-producer` is `1/1 Running` (no more CrashLoopBackOff)
  - `app-queue` (type: `classic`) exists in `/app` vhost with 13 messages and growing
  - All 3 RabbitMQ nodes remain running and clustered
