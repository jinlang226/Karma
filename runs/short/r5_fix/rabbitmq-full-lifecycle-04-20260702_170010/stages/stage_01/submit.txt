**Root cause:** `app-user` was granted only write+read permissions on `/app` — the configure permission was empty (`""`). Declaring (creating) a queue requires configure permission matching the queue name, so RabbitMQ returned "Access refused."

**Fix:** Ran `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` to add the missing configure permission.
