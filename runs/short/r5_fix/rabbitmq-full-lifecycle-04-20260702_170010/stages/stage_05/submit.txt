**What happened under the hood:**

Rolling upgrade was blocked because RabbitMQ 4.x introduces feature flags incompatible with 3.12 (mixed-version clusters are not supported across that gap). The solution was:

1. **Exported** all definitions (vhosts, users, queues, policies) and **captured 2373 messages** from `app-queue` via the management API with `reject_requeue_true` (non-destructive peek)
2. **Scaled the StatefulSet to 0** — clearing the emptyDir volumes (no persistent data to worry about)
3. **Updated the image** to `rabbitmq:4.1-management` and scaled back to 3 replicas — the fresh nodes formed a clean 4.1 cluster
4. **Imported definitions** via `rabbitmqctl import_definitions` and recreated `app-queue` as a classic queue (matching the producer's expectations)
5. **Republished** all 2373 captured messages via the in-cluster `curl-test` pod
