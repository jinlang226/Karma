1. **Added a `rabbitmq` scrape job** targeting each of the 3 pods individually via their headless service DNS names (`rabbitmq-{0,1,2}.rabbitmq-headless.<namespace>.svc.cluster.local:15692`). The recording rule creates `rabbitmq_up` from Prometheus's own per-target `up` metric, which is always 1 when a scrape succeeds.
