All goals are met:

**Root cause:** `app-user` had `write` and `read` permissions on the `/app` vhost but was missing `configure` permission. RabbitMQ requires `configure` to declare (create) a queue — even if it already exists, the AMQP `queue.declare` call checks configure permission.

**Fix:** `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — granted full configure/write/read to `app-user` on `/app`.
