The ETL pipeline completed successfully. Here's a summary of what was done:

**Root Cause Identified:**
- The job had `--driver-memory 256m` and `spark.executor.memory=256m`, but Spark requires at least ~471MB of system memory to initialize. The error was:
  ```
  System memory 259522560 must be at least 471859200
  ```
- Additionally, user_id=999 holds ~50% of records with a 500x skew ratio, making the Stage 3 `GROUP BY user_id` aggregation especially memory-intensive.

**Fix Applied (`etl-job.yaml`):**
1. Raised `--driver-memory` from `256m` → `1g`
2. Raised `spark.executor.memory` from `256m` → `1g`
3. Added `spark.sql.adaptive.enabled=true` and `spark.sql.adaptive.skewJoin.enabled=true` to handle the data skew at partition level
4. Scaled `spark-worker` deployment from 1 → 4 replicas with `SPARK_WORKER_MEMORY=2G`

**Result:** Job status is `Complete 1/1` and the log ends with `spark_etl_pipeline_verified`.
