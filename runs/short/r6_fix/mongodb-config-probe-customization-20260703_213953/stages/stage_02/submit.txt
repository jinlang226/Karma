All done. Here's what was wrong and what was fixed:

**Root cause:** The probes had been tightened to values MongoDB with authentication cannot satisfy:
- `timeoutSeconds: 1` — mongosh + auth handshake takes >1s, causing every probe to time out
- `initialDelaySeconds: 5/15` — replica set initialization needs much more startup time
- `failureThreshold: 2` — pods were killed before they had a chance to stabilize

**Fix applied:** Restored to proper values via `kubectl patch`:

| Probe | Setting | Before | After |
|---|---|---|---|
| readiness | `initialDelaySeconds` | 5 | 30 |
| readiness | `timeoutSeconds` | 1 | 5 |
| readiness | `failureThreshold` | 2 | 6 |
| liveness | `initialDelaySeconds` | 15 | 60 |
| liveness | `timeoutSeconds` | 1 | 5 |
| liveness | `failureThreshold` | 2 | 10 |

**Result:** All 3 pods are `1/1 Ready` with 1 PRIMARY (`mongodb-replica-2`) and 2 SECONDARY members.
