Done. Here's a summary of what was found and fixed:

**Root cause:** The StatefulSet probes had two critical misconfigurations:
- `timeoutSeconds: 1` — too short for `mongosh` to connect and execute, causing every probe to time out
- `failureThreshold: 2` — too low, so pods were killed after just 2 back-to-back timeouts (20s window), before MongoDB had a chance to finish starting
- `initialDelaySeconds` too short (5s readiness, 15s liveness) to allow for normal startup

**Fix applied:** Patched the StatefulSet probes to:
- `timeoutSeconds: 10` (was 1)
- `failureThreshold: 6` (was 2)
- `initialDelaySeconds: 20/60` for readiness/liveness (was 5/15)

**Result:**
- All 3 pods: `1/1 Running` with 0 restarts
- **mongodb-replica-1** — PRIMARY
- **mongodb-replica-0** — SECONDARY
- **mongodb-replica-2** — SECONDARY
