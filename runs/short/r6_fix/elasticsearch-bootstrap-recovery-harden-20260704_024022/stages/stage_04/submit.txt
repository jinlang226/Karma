Everything is working. Here's what was done:

**Root cause:** The StatefulSet mounted no file realm files — the `users` and `users_roles` files in every pod were empty. Both `ops-user` (in `es-file-realm-aggregate`) and `report-user` (in `user-provided-file-realm`) existed only in Kubernetes secrets but were never surfaced to Elasticsearch.

**Fix:**
1. **Merged** the two secrets' `users` and `users_roles` content (ops-user + report-user) into `es-file-realm-aggregate`.
2. **Patched the StatefulSet** to mount `es-file-realm-aggregate` as subPath volume mounts for `/usr/share/elasticsearch/config/users` and `/usr/share/elasticsearch/config/users_roles`.
3. The StatefulSet's rolling update restarted pods one at a time — the cluster stayed green throughout.

**Result:**
- `report-user` authenticates via `default_file` realm with `viewer` role ✓
- `ops-user` authenticates with `superuser` role ✓
- `elastic` built-in user still works ✓
- Cluster is green (3 nodes) ✓
