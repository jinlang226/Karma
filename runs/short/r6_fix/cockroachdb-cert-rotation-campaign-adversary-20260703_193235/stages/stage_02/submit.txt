The cluster is fully operational. Here's what happened and what's now resolved:

**Root cause:** The StatefulSet uses `podManagementPolicy: Parallel`, which launched all 3 pods simultaneously. At startup, pod DNS entries (`crdb-cluster-N.crdb-cluster.cockroachdb.svc.cluster.local`) weren't yet registered in CoreDNS — each pod was trying to reach peers that didn't have resolvable names yet. Node 0 eventually timed out waiting and self-initialized as a single-node cluster; nodes 1 and 2 then resolved DNS and joined it.
