## Results Summary

| Strategy | Elapsed | Optimization | vs Baseline |

**Key observations:**
- The dataset showed a **44x skew ratio** (max partition 11,579 rows vs min 263), caused by 5 hot product_ids receiving 50% of orders.
- **Broadcast join** was the fastest: by broadcasting the small `products` table to every executor, it eliminates the shuffle entirely — no skewed partitions can form.
- **AQE** was nearly as fast: Spark's Adaptive Query Execution detected skewed partitions at runtime and automatically split them, distributing work more evenly without requiring manual hints.
- Both optimizations cut query time roughly in half despite the severe 44x skew in the underlying data distribution.
