The replica set is stable: 1 PRIMARY (`mongodb-replica-2`) and 2 SECONDARY members, all pods Ready.

**What was wrong and what I changed:**

| Probe | Before | After |
|---|---|---|
| Readiness `timeoutSeconds` | `1` | `5` |
| Readiness `initialDelaySeconds` | `5` | `30` |
| Readiness `failureThreshold` | `2` | `6` |
| Readiness eval | `db.hello()` RS-state check | `db.adminCommand("ping")` |
| Liveness `timeoutSeconds` | `1` | `5` |
| Liveness `initialDelaySeconds` | `15` | `60` |
| Liveness `failureThreshold` | `2` | `6` |

The critical fixes:
1. **`timeoutSeconds: 1`** — authenticated `mongosh` routinely takes >1s, causing constant false failures.
2. **`failureThreshold: 2` on liveness** — pods were killed after just 2 slow probe responses, cycling the whole set.
3. **Readiness RS-state check** (`isWritablePrimary || secondary`) — a pod in STARTUP/RECOVERING state fails this, which with `OrderedReady` prevents pod-1 from ever starting since pod-0 can't form a quorum alone. Replaced with a plain `ping`.
