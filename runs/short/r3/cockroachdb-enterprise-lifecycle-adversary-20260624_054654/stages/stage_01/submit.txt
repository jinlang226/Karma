The 3-node CockroachDB cluster is fully deployed and healthy. Here's a summary of what was created:

**Resources deployed:**
- **Headless Service** `crdb-cluster` — DNS-based peer discovery, exposes grpc:26257 + http:8080
- **Public Service** `crdb-cluster-public` — ClusterIP `10.96.105.100`, exposes grpc:26257 + http:8080
- **StatefulSet** `crdb-cluster` — 3/3 replicas Ready, `podManagementPolicy: Parallel` (required so all nodes start together before init)
