All three goals are met. Here's what was wrong and what was fixed:

**Root cause:** `app-user` had write and read permissions on the `/app` vhost but was missing the **configure** permission. RabbitMQ requires configure access to declare (create) a queue — without it, the `queue.declare` AMQP call returns `Access refused`.

**Fix (one command):**
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user '.*' '.*' '.*'
```
This grants `app-user` configure (`.*`), write (`.*`), and read (`.*`) on the `/app` vhost.
