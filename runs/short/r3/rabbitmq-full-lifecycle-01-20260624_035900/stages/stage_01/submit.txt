Everything is working. Here's what was wrong and what I did:

**Root cause:** `app-user` had empty configure permissions on the `/app` vhost. RabbitMQ requires configure permission to declare (create) queues — the producer kept failing with `Access refused` the moment it tried to declare `app-queue`.

**Fix:** Granted `app-user` full configure/write/read permissions on `/app`:
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```

- `app-producer-fc566f6ff-gz2wl` is `1/1 Running`, no restarts
- `app-queue` (classic) in `/app` vhost has 41 messages and growing
