No further probe failures since replica-0 restarted — the crash loop is resolved.

**Root cause:** the probes' `timeoutSeconds: 1` was too short — `mongosh` (a Node-based shell) took ~1.4s just to start, connect with auth, and eval `ping`, so both liveness and readiness probes were timing out and killing containers even though `mongod` itself was healthy.

**Fix applied** (patched the StatefulSet, same exec-based auth-aware probe command, auth left enabled):

| Probe | Before | After |
|---|---|---|
| liveness `timeoutSeconds` | 1 | 5 |
| liveness `initialDelaySeconds` | 15 | 30 |
| liveness `failureThreshold` | 2 | 6 |
| readiness `timeoutSeconds` | 1 | 5 |
| readiness `initialDelaySeconds` | 5 | 15 |
| readiness `failureThreshold` | 2 | 6 |

Rolled the StatefulSet (secondaries first, primary-holder last) to apply. Final state verified:
- All 3 pods `1/1 Ready`, 0 restarts on new config.
- Replica set: `mongodb-replica-1` PRIMARY, `mongodb-replica-0` and `mongodb-replica-2` SECONDARY, all `health: 1`.
