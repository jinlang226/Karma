3. Updated the `es-config` ConfigMap's `discovery.seed_hosts`/`cluster.initial_master_nodes` to list only `es-cluster-0`, matching the intended single-node topology.
4. Scaled back to 1 replica; node came up, self-elected master, `app-data` remained intact and readable.
