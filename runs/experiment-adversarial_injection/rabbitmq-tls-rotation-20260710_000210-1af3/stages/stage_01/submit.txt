Everything is fixed and confirmed:

- **Root cause**: `app-user` had an empty `configure` permission regex on the `/app` vhost (`admin` was fully permissioned, but `app-user` only had `write`/`read`), so it was denied when declaring `app-queue`.
- **Fix**: `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — no operator/CRD changes, no data loss (existing vhost/data untouched).
- **Verified**: `app-producer` pod is now `1/1 Running`, and `app-queue` (type `classic`) in `/app` has 59 messages and growing. All 3 RabbitMQ nodes remain `Running`.
