Both cases work as expected.

**Root cause:** The `canary-canary` Ingress (the canary Ingress for `canary.example.com` → `canary-echo`) had `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/canary-by-header-value: wrong` instead of `always`, so the header match never triggered and all traffic fell through to the stable Ingress (`canary-stable`). There was also an unrelated decoy Ingress (`canary-alt`, for `canary-alt.example.com`) using `beta` as its header value — not part of this routing and left untouched.

**Fix:** Updated the annotation to `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/canary-by-header-value: always` on `canary-canary`.

**Verified:**
- No header → `stable`
- `X-Canary: always` → `canary`

No Deployments or Services were modified.
