Fixed. The root cause: the health probe script (`probe.sh`) does a `sleep 2` plus a `mongosh` auth connection, but the probes were configured with `timeoutSeconds: 1`, so every probe was guaranteed to time out — causing constant liveness-triggered restarts before the replica set could ever stabilize.

I patched the StatefulSet's probes to give the script realistic headroom:
- **readinessProbe**: `initialDelaySeconds: 20, periodSeconds: 15, timeoutSeconds: 10, failureThreshold: 5`
- **livenessProbe**: `initialDelaySeconds: 60, periodSeconds: 20, timeoutSeconds: 10, failureThreshold: 6`

Auth (`--auth` + keyfile) was left untouched. After recreating the pods, all 3 are `1/1 Running` with 0 restarts, and `rs.status()` confirms 1 PRIMARY + 2 SECONDARY.
