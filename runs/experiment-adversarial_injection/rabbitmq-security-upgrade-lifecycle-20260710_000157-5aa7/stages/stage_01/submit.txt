All goals are met:

- **Root cause**: `app-user` had permissions on the `/app` vhost with an empty `configure` regex (`user configure write read` → `app-user \t .* .*`), so it was denied when declaring (`configure`-ing) `app-queue`, causing `app-producer` to crash-loop with `not_authorised`.
- **Fix**: ran `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` on `rabbitmq-0` to grant `app-user` configure/write/read on `/app` — a targeted permission fix with no data loss and no CRDs/operators involved.
