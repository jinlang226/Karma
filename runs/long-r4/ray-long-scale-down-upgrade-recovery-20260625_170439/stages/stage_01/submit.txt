In Ray 2.9.0, `ray.init` with a GCS address requires a local raylet to be running on the client pod — it doesn't auto-start one cleanly without the session being pre-established. Running `ray start --address=ray-head:6379` on the ray-client pod joined it to the cluster, after which `ray.init` found the existing local session and connected successfully.
