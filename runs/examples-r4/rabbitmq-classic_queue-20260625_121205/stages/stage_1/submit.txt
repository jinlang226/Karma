All goals are met:

**Root cause:** `app-user` had `write` and `read` permissions on the `/app` vhost but an empty `configure` pattern. RabbitMQ requires `configure` permission to declare (create) a queue — without it, the AMQP `queue.declare` call returns "Access refused."

**Fix:** Granted `app-user` full permissions on `/app`:
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```

- `app-producer` is `1/1 Running`
- `/app` vhost contains `app-queue` of type `classic` with 11 messages (and growing)
