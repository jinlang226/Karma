Key details:

- A `PodDisruptionBudget` with `minAvailable: 2` ensures voluntary disruptions preserve quorum
- All pods carry both required labels (`app.kubernetes.io/name: cockroachdb` and `app.kubernetes.io/instance: crdb-cluster`)
