**What happened and why:**

The upgrade path was `3.9 → 3.11 → 3.12 → 3.13 → 4.0 → 4.1` — not a direct 3.9→3.12 jump. RabbitMQ 3.12 requires the `classic_mirrored_queue_version` feature flag to be enabled before upgrade, but that flag was only introduced in 3.10. So the necessary intermediate step at **3.11** was to enable all feature flags (including `classic_mirrored_queue_version`) before proceeding.

A recovery detour was required: the first attempt to jump 3.9→3.12 directly caused a CrashLoopBackOff, and the StatefulSet entered an OrderedReady deadlock (pod-0 waited for Mnesia quorum, pods-1/2 waited for pod-0 to be Ready first). Breaking it required `rabbitmqctl force_boot` on the running-but-not-ready pod-0 to designate it as the cluster leader on next restart.
