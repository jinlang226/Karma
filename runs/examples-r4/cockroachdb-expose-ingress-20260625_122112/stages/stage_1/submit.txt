Both are working:

- **HTTPS 443**: returns `<!DOCTYPE html>` CockroachDB Console — `https://crdb-ui.example.com/` routes to `crdb-cluster-public:8080`

Here's a summary of what was configured:

- `Ingress/crdb-ui` in `cockroachdb` — nginx ingress class, terminates TLS, backends to `crdb-cluster-public:8080`

**SQL TCP passthrough**
- `ConfigMap/ingress-nginx-tcp-services` in `ingress-nginx` — maps port `26257` → `cockroachdb/crdb-cluster-public:26257`
- Patched `Deployment/ingress-nginx-controller` — added `--tcp-services-configmap` arg and port 26257
- Patched `Service/ingress-nginx-controller` — added port 26257 (NodePort 32479) to the LoadBalancer service
