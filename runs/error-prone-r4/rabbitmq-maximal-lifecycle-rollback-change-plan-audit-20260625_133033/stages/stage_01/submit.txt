All goals met:

- **Root cause**: `app-user` had empty configure permission on the `/app` vhost. Declaring a queue requires configure access (`".*"`), but only write+read were set.
- **Fix**: `rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"` — grants full configure/write/read to `app-user` on `/app`.
- **Result**: `app-producer` is `1/1 Running`, `app-queue` (classic) exists in `/app` with messages accumulating (15 and growing), all 3 RabbitMQ nodes remain healthy.
