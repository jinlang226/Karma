**Why `ray start` was needed on the client:**  
In Ray 2.9.0, `ray.init(address='host:port')` runs in driver mode — it expects the calling node to already be registered as a raylet in the cluster. Running `ray start --address=ray-head:6379` on the ray-client pod registers it as a worker node so the driver can connect. The pod itself was not modified.
