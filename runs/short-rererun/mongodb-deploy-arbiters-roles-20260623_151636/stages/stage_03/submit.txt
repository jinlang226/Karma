The fix is complete. Here's what was done:

1. **Root cause**: `reporting-user` had only the `rawRead` role (granting `find` on `appdb.raw`), with no access to `appdb.reports` — the wrong collection was exposed.

2. **Fix**:
   - Created a new custom role `reportsRead` in `appdb` with a single privilege: `find` on `appdb.reports` only.
   - Updated `reporting-user` to have `reportsRead` instead of `rawRead`.

3. **Verification**: `reporting-user` can now count documents in `appdb.reports` (returns 3) and gets `Unauthorized` when accessing `appdb.raw`.
