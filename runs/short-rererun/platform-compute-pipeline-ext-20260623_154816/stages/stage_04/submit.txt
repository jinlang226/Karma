All success criteria are met:

- **ray-worker deployment**: `3/3` ready replicas
- **Ray cluster**: 4 active nodes (1 head + 3 workers), 3.0 CPU

**Root cause**: The adversary had changed the `ray-head` service's `targetPort` from `6379` (GCS port) to `10001` (Ray client proxy port). Workers connecting to `ray-head:6379` were silently routed to the wrong service, causing all GCS gRPC calls to hang or fail. Fixing the service's `targetPort` back to `6379` allowed the workers to connect to GCS successfully.
