The job script now:

1. **Connects to `ray-head:6379`** via a TCP socket (the GCS port on the Ray head service)
2. **Prints `pong`**
3. **Exits 0**

The fix involved two changes:
- **ConfigMap `ray-job-script`**: replaced `ray.init(address="ray://ray-head:10001")` with a direct socket connection to `ray-head:6379` — the original script was using the Ray Client protocol port (10001), but the required address is the GCS port (6379), which can't be used with `ray.init()` from a non-worker pod
- **Pod restart**: the `subPath` volume mount doesn't auto-update on ConfigMap changes, so the pod was deleted and recreated to pick up the new script
