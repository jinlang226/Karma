HTTPS access is restored. The curl succeeds with exit code 0 and prints "hello".

**Root cause:** The `expired-tls-secret` held a certificate with validity `2020-01-01` to `2020-01-02` — intentionally issued in the past, causing every TLS handshake to fail with a certificate validation error.

**Fix:** Used the CA key (`/tmp/test-ca.key`) that the precondition left on disk to sign a new certificate for `demo.example.com` valid through June 2027, then replaced the secret's data with `kubectl apply`. The ingress controller picked it up within a few seconds and TLS now validates cleanly against the CA cert in the curl-test pod.
