Split-horizon is working correctly. The external client connecting via the external hostnames receives `domain-rs-1:31181`, `domain-rs-2:31182`, `domain-rs-3:31183` in the `isMaster` response — not the internal cluster DNS names.

Updated the `mongodb-tls-cert` secret and rolled the StatefulSet.

3. **Configured split-horizon** via `rs.reconfig()` on the primary, adding a `horizons.external` entry to each member:
   - member 0 → `domain-rs-1:31181`
   - member 1 → `domain-rs-2:31182`
   - member 2 → `domain-rs-3:31183`
