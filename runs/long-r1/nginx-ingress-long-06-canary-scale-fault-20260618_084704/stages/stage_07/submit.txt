Both pass.

- **No header** → `stable` ✓
- **`X-Canary: always`** → `canary` ✓

## What was wrong

The canary Ingress `canary-canary` (host `canary.example.com` → `canary-echo`) had the right `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/canary: "true"` flag, but its matching annotations were misconfigured:

- `canary-by-header: X-Feature` (should be `X-Canary`)
- `canary-by-header-value: v2` (should be `always`)

So no request ever matched the canary condition, and everything fell through to the stable backend.

## Fix

Updated the two annotations on `canary-canary`:
```
nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/canary-by-header=X-Canary
nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/canary-by-header-value=always
```

I left the `canary-alt` Ingress alone (it's a labeled decoy: `benchmark.decoy=true`, different host `canary-alt.example.com`) and made no changes to any Deployments or Services.
