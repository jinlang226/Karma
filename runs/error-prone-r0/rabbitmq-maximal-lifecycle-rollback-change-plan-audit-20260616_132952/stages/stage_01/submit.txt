All goals are met. Here's a summary of what was wrong and what was fixed:

**Root cause:** `app-user` had missing **configure** permission on the `/app` vhost (`"" ".*" ".*"`). In AMQP, declaring a queue requires configure permission — write/read alone aren't enough. The producer was failing immediately on `queue.declare` with `Access refused`.

**Fix:** Granted full permissions to `app-user` on `/app`:
```
rabbitmqctl set_permissions -p /app app-user ".*" ".*" ".*"
```
