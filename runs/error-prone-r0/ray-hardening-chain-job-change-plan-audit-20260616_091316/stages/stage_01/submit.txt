Everything is running. Here's what was done:

1. **Deployed** `ray-head` (Deployment, 1 replica), `ray-head` Service, and `ray-worker` (Deployment, 2 replicas) from the provided manifests into the `ray` namespace.

2. **Root cause of validation failure**: In Ray 2.9.0, `ray.init(address='...')` expects a local raylet already running on the calling node. The ray-client had a stale `/tmp/ray` session with only a lock file and no `node_ip_address.json`, causing it to hang waiting. Running `ray start --address=ray-head:6379` on the ray-client joined it to the cluster as a proper node, after which `ray.init` connected immediately and printed `ok`.
