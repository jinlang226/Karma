Everything is stable. Here's a summary of what was wrong and what was fixed:

**Root cause:** The `health-overrides` ConfigMap had a stale entry `mongodb-replica-1: legacyHealthPass123`. The health-check script loads per-pod password overrides from this ConfigMap, so every probe on `mongodb-replica-1` (the PRIMARY) was attempting to authenticate with the wrong password, causing repeated `Authentication failed` probe failures.

**Fixes applied:**

1. **`health-overrides` ConfigMap** — removed the stale `mongodb-replica-1: legacyHealthPass123` entry. The health check now falls back to the correct `healthpass123` password from the `health-user-password` secret for all pods.

2. **Probe `timeoutSeconds` 5→10** — increased on both liveness and readiness probes in the StatefulSet to eliminate the intermittent timeout failures seen on `mongodb-replica-2` (mongosh startup + auth can push against a 5s limit).

**Final state:**
- All 3 pods: `1/1 Running`, 0 restarts
- Replica set: 1 PRIMARY (`mongodb-replica-1`) + 2 SECONDARY (`mongodb-replica-0`, `mongodb-replica-2`)
