**Fix applied (3 changes, no operators/CRDs):**

3. **Updated the `elasticsearch` StatefulSet** — added the `es-file-realm-aggregate` secret as a volume and mounted it as `users`, `users_roles`, and `roles.yml` at `/usr/share/elasticsearch/config/`. This triggered a rolling restart (all 3 pods cycled one at a time, cluster stayed green throughout).

After restart: `report-user` authenticates via the file realm, `ops-user` does too, the `elastic` native user still works, and the seeded `app-data` index is readable by `report-user`.
