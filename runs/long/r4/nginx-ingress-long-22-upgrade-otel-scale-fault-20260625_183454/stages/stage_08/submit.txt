Here's what was wrong and what I fixed:

**Root cause — two issues:**

1. **Expired TLS certificate** — `expired-tls-secret` contained a cert valid only from Jan 1–2, 2020 (expired >6 years ago). I generated a new 2-year cert for `demo.example.com` signed by the Test CA (`/tmp/test-ca.key` + `/tmp/test-ca.crt`) and updated the secret.

2. **Wrong IngressClass** — `demo-ingress` referenced `ingressClassName: nginx`, but after the nginx ingress upgrade, no controller handles the `nginx` IngressClass. The active controller on port 30108 uses `ingress-1`. I patched the ingress to `ingressClassName: ingress-1`.

**Verification:** `curl --cacert /tmp/tls/ca.crt` returns HTTP 200 with body `hello`, `SSL certificate verify ok`, certificate CN=demo.example.com valid until Jun 24 2028.
