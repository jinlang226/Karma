**Summary of what was wrong and what was fixed:**

- **Root cause:** The `health-overrides` ConfigMap contained a stale entry `mongodb-replica-1: legacyHealthPass123`. The health check script on `mongodb-replica-1` used this override password to authenticate, but MongoDB's `health-user` account has password `healthpass123`. This caused every readiness probe on `mongodb-replica-1` to fail with an authentication error.

- **Fix:** Removed the stale `mongodb-replica-1` entry from the `health-overrides` ConfigMap, then restarted the pod to pick up the change immediately. The pod came up healthy on the next probe cycle.

- **Result:** All 3 pods are `1/1 Ready`, and the replica set has 1 PRIMARY + 2 SECONDARY with all members at `health: 1`.
