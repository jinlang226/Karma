Both routes work correctly:

- **Without header** → `stable`
- **With `X-Canary: always`** → `canary`

**Root cause:** The `canary-canary` ingress had its annotations tampered — `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/canary-by-header` was changed from `X-Canary` to `X-Feature` and `canary-by-header-value` from `always` to `v2`. The patch restored them to the correct values so nginx-ingress now matches the intended header and routes accordingly.
