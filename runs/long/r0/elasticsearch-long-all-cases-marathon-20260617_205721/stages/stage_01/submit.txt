**What was done:**

1. **Root cause**: The ConfigMap was missing `cluster.initial_master_nodes`, required in ES 7.x to bootstrap a fresh cluster. Without it, no node knows which peers are eligible to seed the master election.

2. **Fix**: Added `cluster.initial_master_nodes: [es-cluster-0, es-cluster-1, es-cluster-2]` to the ConfigMap and rolled the StatefulSet to apply it. The cluster formed immediately.

3. **Bootstrap setting removed**: After the cluster formed, `cluster.initial_master_nodes` was removed from the ConfigMap (it must not persist — if left in, it can cause split-brain on future full restarts).

4. **Rolling restart verified safe**: Each pod was restarted one at a time; the cluster stayed green throughout because the remaining 2/3 master-eligible nodes always maintained quorum.
