**Summary of what was done:**

1. **Created missing resources** — the precondition probe found `curl-test` already running (from a prior stage) and skipped the apply step, so `rate-echo` deployment/service and both Ingresses for `rate.example.com` were never created. Applied them from the case resource files.

2. **Fixed adversary fault** — the `scale_down_backend` adversary had scaled `demo-app` to 0 replicas; scaled it back to 1.

3. **Added rate limiting** — annotated the `rate-api` Ingress with `limit-rps: "1"` and `limit-burst-multiplier: "1"`. The default burst multiplier of 5 would have let all 10 test requests through; reducing it to 1 (burst=1) produces ~4 rejections per 10 requests at 2 QPS.

4. **Configured 429 status** — patched the `ingress-nginx-controller` ConfigMap with `limit-req-status-code: "429"`. The `rate-health` Ingress has no rate-limit annotations, so it remains fully unlimited.
