Enable TLS for the running MongoDB replica set.

The openssl-toolbox pod is available in the mongodb namespace to generate
certificates. Mount certificates into mongo-rs pods at /etc/tls with:

- /etc/tls/ca.crt
- /etc/tls/server.pem
- /etc/tls/client.pem

Plain (non-TLS) connections must fail after the change, and TLS connections
using the files above must succeed.
