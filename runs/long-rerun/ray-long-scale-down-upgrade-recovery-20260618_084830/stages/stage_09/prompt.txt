A Ray cluster is running, but the worker deployment only has 3 replica.

Your task: scale the worker deployment to 4 replicas.

Recommended commands:
```bash
kubectl -n ray scale deploy/ray-worker --replicas=4
kubectl -n ray rollout status deploy/ray-worker --timeout=120s
```

Success criteria: ray-worker shows 4 ready replicas and the Ray cluster
reports 4 live nodes (1 head + 4 workers).
