ES 7.x refuses to bootstrap a fresh cluster without that setting, so no master was ever elected → endless "master not discovered yet", pods stuck `0/1`.

2. Restarted the three pods → cluster bootstrapped, master elected, **green** with 3 nodes.
3. **Removed** `cluster.initial_master_nodes` from the ConfigMap (it's bootstrap-only; leaving it in is a split-brain/re-bootstrap hazard on future restarts).
4. Performed an ordered rolling restart (es-cluster-2 → 1 → 0), confirming the cluster re-elected a master and stayed healthy at each step **without** the bootstrap setting.
