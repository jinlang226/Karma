Both criteria are met:
- Without `X-Feature` header → `stable`
- With `X-Feature: v2` → `canary`

**What was wrong and what I fixed:**

The `canary-canary` Ingress had its annotations misconfigured — it was matching on `X-Canary: beta` instead of `X-Feature: v2`. I patched two annotations with `kubectl annotate --overwrite`:

| Annotation | Before | After |
|---|---|---|
| `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/canary-by-header` | `X-Canary` | `X-Feature` |
| `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/canary-by-header-value` | `beta` | `v2` |

The `nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/canary: "true"` annotation was already present, so nginx-ingress treats this Ingress as a canary overlay on top of `canary-stable`. Requests matching the header go to `canary-echo`; all others fall through to `stable-echo`.
